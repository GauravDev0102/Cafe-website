Our Story

Rooted in Craft,
Driven by Flavour

Brewed Haven started as a weekend pop-up stall in 2015. Today it's a full café powered by a team of passionate baristas obsessed with quality.

We source our beans directly from Coorg, Araku Valley, and Wayanad — building relationships with farmers who share our commitment to sustainable farming.

  • 100% ethically sourced single-origin beans
  • Freshly baked pastries made every morning
  • Free Wi-Fi & quiet corners for focused work
  • Private dining room available for events
